Bars

Prieplaukos Vynine

Located in the harbour, and overlooking the lagoon and dunes, this classy wine bar is a popular spot! It is also renowned for its live music and jam sessions, featuring international artists during the Nida Jazz festival

Bo House

This chilled out funky place is perfect for when you want to grab a bite. It also has a great atmosphere for drinks in the evening, especially when the basketball (Lithuania's second religion) is on!

Beach Bars

Next to a beach voleyball court, with many colourful beanbags and chilled out tunes, these bars are a place to watch the sunset and dance until the dawn. Walk down the beach to find them!
