Things to do

Nida Jazz

This yearly 3 day Summer Jazz festival features artists from France, Portugal, Austria, USA, Germany and many more countries, giving it a truly colourful line up. 2017 Nida Jazz on 11-13th of August promises heartwarming vibes and beautiful sunsets in a paradise-like location

Beachball FEST

Sign up with your friends as a team in this Volleyball tournament, organised each month of the Summer, and compete at an amateur level, with groovy tunes all day long. Beach stage with DJ and artist performances, break dance competition and good vibes until the dawn are waiting for you this summer

Parnidis Dune

Head to the Parnidis Dune for a walk along the thread of golden dunes and witness the stunning panorama of the Curonian Spit, where you can see the Baltic Sea and Curonian Lagoon simultaneously, standing on a land between two waters
