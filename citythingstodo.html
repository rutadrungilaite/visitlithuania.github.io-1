Things to do

St Anne's Church

The church of St Anne is a masterpiece of the late Gothic period. Popular legend has it that Napoleon Bonaparte, who was fascinated by the beauty of the church, wanted to take it back to Paris in the palm of his hand

Pilies Street

The aforementioned Pilies street is the oldest street of Vilnius and one that leads into the old town and across it, bearing a lot of historical and cultural significance

Vilnius University Botanic Gradens

Vilnius University Botanical Garden (VUBG) was founded in 1781. Today the Garden resides in two areas, Vingis Park and Kairėnai Estate, and is not only the largest in Lithuania but also have the most numerous collections of plants: growing here are about 10,000 taxa of plants belonging to 220 families and 1120 genera
