Nida Jazz

This yearly 3 day Summer Jazz festival features artists from France, Portugal, Austria, USA, Germany and many more countries, giving it a truly colourful line up. 2017 Nida Jazz on 11-13th of August promises heartwarming vibes and beautiful sunsets in a paradise-like location
